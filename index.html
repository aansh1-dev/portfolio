Primary UX Research | April 2026

Swiggy vs Zomato
UX Case Study

The Objective

I placed 1 order (Domino's Big Big 6in1 Cheeseburst, ₹999) on both Swiggy and Zomato and captured every step of the flow. Here is the data:

1 / 4 • Audit Evidence

01 Price Transparency

• Swiggy checkout: ₹1,111 final (₹111.63 added as "GST & Other Charges")

• Zomato checkout: ₹1,113.63 final (itemised breakdown provided)

Outcome: Exposed 11.2% hidden inflation on Swiggy at checkout.

02 Coupon UX

• Swiggy: Displayed 3 ineligible coupons, increasing spend friction.

• Zomato: Auto-applied discount and itemized exact savings.

Redesign Recommendations

Rec 1

Shipped redesign: show only applicable coupons at checkout.

Rec 2

Audited line items: break down "GST & Other Charges".

Rec 3

Drove clarity: reduce competitor ad density in direct searches.

Rec 4

Led transparency: show estimated final price on restaurant page.
