UX Audit | April 2026

Swiggy vs Zomato
UX Case Study

The Objective

I placed the same Domino's order on Swiggy and Zomato simultaneously and captured 13 live checkout screenshots to audit pricing transparency and coupon friction.

1 / 4 • Audit Evidence

01 Price Transparency

• Swiggy checkout: ₹1,111 final (₹111.63 added as "GST & Other Charges")

• Zomato checkout: ₹1,113.63 final (itemised breakdown provided)

Findings: Swiggy added ₹111 in undisclosed charges vs Zomato's itemized breakdown.

02 Coupon UX

• Swiggy: Displayed 3 ineligible coupons, increasing spend friction.

• Zomato: Auto-applied discount and itemized exact savings per code.

Redesign Recommendations

Rec 1

Show only applicable coupons at checkout to reduce decision friction.

Rec 2

Break down "GST & Other Charges" into line items at the cart stage.

Rec 3

Remove competitor ads from direct branded searches.

Rec 4

Show estimated final price on the restaurant page, before checkout.
